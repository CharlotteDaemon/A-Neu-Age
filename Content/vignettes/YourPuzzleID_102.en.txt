#Vignettes Files

#Vignette files are used to define dialogues and book entries. Basic features like cutscene transisitions and text formatting can be seen by looking at vignettes in the original campaign.

Book entries don't have characters, and extra newlines are used to make paragraphs.
This line is an example of when you use only one newline.

This line is shows what happens when you use two.


And this line demonstrates three newlines.

Cutscenes and documents also use vignette files, though the formatting is different, and Quintessential doesn't support them for campaigns yet, I think.
